CITTIC

CITTIC is the first university centric student TBI in the state. The 4000 sq ft space offer excellent environment to our faculty and students who have brilliant ideas / research output which may eventually be commercialized. Incubation facilities include air-conditioned co-working space, high speed wifi network, meeting rooms etc offered at minimal charges. With the objective to promote the entrepreneurial culture among students from the start of their study programs to change their mindset from job seeker to job provider CITTIC regularly organize entrepreneurial awareness programmes and expert talks to create a culture of innovation and to spread the spirit of start-up and entrepreneurship. Among the events organized by CITTIC in the campus, the promi- nent ones include CUSAT INNOVATE, MAKER WEEKEND, HACKATHONS, ENTREPRENEUR- SHIP AWARENESS CAMP (EAC) etc. CUSAT has implemented the Student Entrepreneurship policy by which students registering start-ups with CITTIC are eligible for grace mark up to 4% and attendance up to 20%. More than 100 students have availed this across 3 years.

CUSAT TBI

In line with the government policy of encouraging university drives startup ecosystem,CUSAT set up its frist Technology Business Incubator named Center for Innovation, Technology Transfer & Indus- trial Collaboration (CITTIC) in 2011 to promote the entrepreneurial culture among our stakeholders.
