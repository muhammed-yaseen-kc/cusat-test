RUSA INNOVATION AND STARTUP ECOSYSTEM

With financial support from RUSA,CUSAT has set up RUSA Innovation Startup Ecosystem (RISE) mainly for electronics and biotech startups. Rise also proudly possesses a bio incubator with necessary equipment and facilities and a FABLAB sponsored by Kerala Startup Mission. Another 10000 sq ft furnished space is being added to boost the physical infrastructural facilities of the TBI.

CUSAT TBI

In line with the government policy of encouraging university drives startup ecosystem,CUSAT set up its frist Technology Business Incubator named Center for Innovation, Technology Transfer & Indus- trial Collaboration (CITTIC) in 2011 to promote the entrepreneurial culture among our stakeholders.
