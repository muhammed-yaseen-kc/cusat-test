BIO INCUBATOR

In the form of a business centre, the TBI provides incubatees an initial place of operation equipped with supportive environment in which services such as assistance in technology, hands-on management, mentoring, business plan development, exposure to Venture Capital and Institutional Capital and all other operational business/finance related needs involved in starting and growing a technology company.


INCUBATION

CUSAT TBI promotes the concept of growth through innovations and applications of technology, support, economic development strategies for Small Business Development.
